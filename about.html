Our Mission

Our mission is to worship God faithfully in the Syriac Orthodox tradition, to preserve our sacred heritage, and to nurture spiritual growth within our community.

Our Faith

We belong to the Jacobite Syrian Orthodox Church, part of the ancient Oriental Orthodox communion tracing its roots to the Apostolic See of Antioch.

Our worship follows the West Syriac Rite, rich in prayer, hymnody, scripture, and sacramental life.
