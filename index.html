Welcome to Our Church

We are a vibrant Jacobite Syrian Orthodox community in Sheffield, worshipping in the ancient faith and tradition of the Holy Apostolic Church. Rooted in the West Syriac liturgical heritage, we gather to celebrate the Holy Qurbono and grow together in Christ.
